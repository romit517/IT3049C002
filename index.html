How do you win at Hangman???


To play Hangman, start by selecting a secret word. Next, draw a blank line for each letter of the word on a piece of paper. Then, have the other players guess letters, one at a time, to figure out the secret word. When the players guess a letter correctly, fill the letter into the corresponding blank.
